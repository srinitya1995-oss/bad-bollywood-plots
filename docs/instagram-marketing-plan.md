# Bad Desi Plots — Instagram Marketing Plan

> Anonymous, brand-only account. Voice = the cards. Targets diaspora + India in parallel. Cadence: 5–7 posts/week (~30 min/day). Goal: drive WAU on baddesiplots.com via shareable card content.

---

## 1. North Star

**One job:** turn each card into a thumb-stopping "I know this one!" moment that earns a comment, a save, a share, and (eventually) a tap to the link in bio.

**Strategic bet:** the cards themselves are already designed to be screenshots. The terrible-plot-summary format IS the hook. We don't need a face, a founder story, or a brand voice on top of it. The product is the meme.

**12-week target (organic only):**
- Followers: **2,500–5,000**
- Saves per post (median): **80+**
- DMs/comments asking "where can I play this": **20+/week**
- Site click-through from bio link: **8–12%** of profile visits
- WAU on baddesiplots.com: **400+** (vs ~86 lifetime today)

## 3. Visual System

Every post lives in one of three templates. Consistent template = recognizable feed = stronger algorithm signal.

### Template A — "The Card" (single card, the workhorse)
- 4:5 portrait (1080×1350)
- Card centered on `--bg` with a `--gold` jharokha-arch border
- Card front face only (front face = terrible plot)
- Difficulty dot (easy / medium / hard) top-right
- BW orange glow OR TW emerald glow — never mix
- Bottom corner watermark: `baddesiplots.com` in DM Sans 12pt, `--cream` 60% opacity
- Caption: 1-line hook + "guess in comments 👇 reveal tomorrow"

### Template B — "Carousel of 5" (the saver)
- 5-slide carousel, 4:5
- Slide 1: cover — "5 plots. how many can you guess?"
- Slides 2–5: one card each (front face only)
- Slide 6 (final): "drop your score 👇 link in bio for the answers"
- Caption ends with: "save this for your next chai session"

### Template C — "Reveal" (the punchline)
- 2-slide carousel
- Slide 1: front face (plot)
- Slide 2: back face (movie name + year + cast hint)
- Use Framer-style transition feel by mimicking the 0.4s overshoot in the swipe arrow animation
- Always posted ~24h after the matching Template A or B post

**Visual tokens (use exactly):**
- BG: `#1A0F0A`
- BW accent: `#E85D3A` + glow rgba(232,93,58,0.35)
- TW accent: `#3EA87A` + glow rgba(62,168,122,0.35)
- Gold: `#F2A72E`
- Cream (text): `#F5E8D4`
- Display font: Playfair Display
- Body/caption-overlay font: DM Sans

**Banned in feed:** pure white backgrounds, sterile minimalism, gradient-only ornaments, mixing BW + TW glows in the same post, gold for navigation chrome.

## 6. Ten Reel Concepts (ready to shoot)

Each reel: **9–18s**, hook in first 1.5s, hard cut at the end, no slow intros.

1. **"Bollywood narrator reads a plot"** — text-to-speech with a deep AI voice (ElevenLabs "Adam" or similar Hindi-accented voice) reading the worst plot in your deck. Slow Ken-Burns on the card. Reveal at end.

2. **"Auntie summarizes the movie"** — AI voice or your own voiceover in caricatured-auntie tone summarizing a movie like she barely watched it. Card on screen, voice on top.

3. **"Subway POV, you're playing this"** — phone screen recording of someone playing on baddesiplots.com, ambient subway audio, captions over the screen. Anonymous (only hands visible).

4. **"5 cards in 15 seconds"** — rapid card flipper. Each card on screen for 3 seconds with a "ding" SFX. Final frame: "play full game ↓"

5. **"Difficulty progression"** — easy → medium → hard cards back-to-back, tension builds via tempo of cuts and music, ends with a near-impossible hard card.

6. **"Wrong answer reactions"** — caption: "when your cousin guesses *Sholay* for every card." Card flips to reveal a totally different movie. Use comedic Bollywood SFX.

7. **"BW vs TW"** — 1 BW card and 1 TW card with the same trope. Caption: "same plot, different industry. guess both."

8. **"Decoder challenge"** — overlay caption: "this plot describes ____" with a 5-second timer overlay. Sweat drip emoji. Reveal.

9. **"Family WhatsApp group reacts"** — fake screenshot of a family group chat losing it over guessing a card. Animated typing dots, names blurred. Funny at the diaspora-aunt level.

10. **"Wrong year"** — caption "this movie came out the year you were born." Card → reveal year. Plays on nostalgia. Good engagement bait for "OMG same."

**Reel post-time:** 7–10pm IST (catches India primetime + US morning).

## 9. Growth Tactics (cold start, weeks 1–4)

### Engagement loops (free, do daily)
1. **Reply to every single comment in the first 60 minutes** for the first 30 days. The algo treats this as session length.
2. **Comment on 10 desi meme accounts/day** with witty replies (not "great post 🙌"). Builds discovery.
3. **DM 3 people/day** who liked similar accounts: "made you something — try this game free, no signup."
4. **Repost any tagged stories within 1 hour.** Reciprocity = retagging = network effect.

### Collabs (the lever for cold-start)
**Target list (DM these in week 2):**
- Mid-size desi meme pages: search `bollywood meme account`, `tollywood memes`, `desi diaspora memes` — aim for 10k–80k followers (anything bigger ignores cold DMs).
- Bollywood quiz pages on IG (there are ~20 active ones).
- Trivia/party-game adjacent pages.

**The pitch (under 5 lines):**
```
hey — built a free desi party game where you guess movies from terrible plots.
no signup, plays in browser, your followers will go feral.
made you a 5-card carousel branded for your page if you want to repost — no ask.
[card pack link]
```

The unbranded card pack costs you nothing. If 1 in 10 reposts, that's 100k+ free reach.

### Community seeding
- Post a card daily in: r/Bollywood, r/IndianTeenagers, r/IndiaSpeaks, r/IndianGaming, desi Discord servers, family WhatsApp groups (yes, really).
- Ask 5 friends to share to one IG story each on launch day. That's 5×500 avg story views = 2,500 first impressions, free.

### Paid (start week 4 only)
- $20 boost on best-performing organic reel
- Audience: India + US/UK/CA, 18–34, interests: Bollywood, Tollywood, party games, board games
- Optimization: profile visits (not link clicks — too early)
- Kill if CPM > $8

## 11. The Don't List

- **Don't use the founder voice.** No "I built this" posts. The brand is faceless.
- **Don't hardcode hex codes wrong.** Every post uses tokens from `style.css`. Cream not white, ink not black.
- **Don't cross-glow.** BW posts use orange glow only, TW posts use emerald glow only.
- **Don't post reveals before 24 hours.** Engagement = the time gap between hook and reveal.
- **Don't post more than 1 reveal per day.** Saves the punchline.
- **Don't follow random accounts to inflate.** Anonymous brand accounts following 2k people look like bots and get shadowbanned.
- **Don't reply with founder-voice on comments.** Always reply *as the brand*: short, witty, in the voice of the cards themselves.
- **Don't spell the answers in alt text.** Some users reveal-cheat by reading alt text. Keep it generic ("worn paper card with a Bollywood movie plot").
- **Don't post on Sundays before 6pm.** Lowest engagement window for desi audiences.
- **Don't run a paid campaign before week 4.** No data, no targets, no point.
