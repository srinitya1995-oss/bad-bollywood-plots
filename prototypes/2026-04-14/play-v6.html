How to play

  1. Pick Solo or Pass & Play. Pass & Play is for 2 to 8 people sharing one phone.
  2. Read the terrible plot on the card out loud. You get 15 seconds in your head to guess the movie.
  3. Tap to flip. If you nailed the title, tap Got it. If you missed, tap Missed.
  4. In Pass & Play the phone goes to the next player after every card. Whoever has the most points when the deck ends wins.
