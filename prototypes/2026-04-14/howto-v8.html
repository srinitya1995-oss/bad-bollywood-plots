Bad Desi Plots
How To Play
The Rules 2 Min Read
1

Read the bad plot

Tap Flip to reveal a terribly summarized Bollywood or Tollywood movie. Read it out loud.

2

Guess the movie

First player to shout the correct movie name earns the points. No googling, no half-guesses, only confidence.

3

Pass the phone

When a plot is solved, hand the phone to the next player. They flip, you guess. Rotate until time's up.

4

Skip freely

Stuck? Skip loses you nothing except bragging rights. A round is 12 plots or six minutes, whichever hits first.
