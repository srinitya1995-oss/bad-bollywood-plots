Story Highlight Covers — set of 6

Each cover is 1080×1080 (renders inside IG's circle mask at ~80px). Same architecture across the set — ornament top, icon center, label bottom, gold ring around — so they read as a system at the top of the profile.

Choices

Bordered gold ring at 920px diameter: 4px gold stroke + 14px gold halo + dashed inner ring. The ring is the constant visual signature. Each cover gets one cream-stroke icon: Plays = play triangle, Reveals = eye, Cards = stacked rectangles, How to Play = info dot, Submissions = paper plane, Behind = curtain. Stroke icons (not filled) so they read at small sizes. One small flame-orange accent per icon — a single dot or stroke that grounds the icon in the brand. Crown ornament top + paisley ornament bottom — symmetric so all six covers read as a set. Label in DM Sans 30px tracked +0.55em. Italic gold subtitle below in Playfair italic — adds the voice to the system without making it cluttered. No pure white anywhere — icons are cream stroke, not white.

Squint test

At ~80px circle thumbnail, the icon is the load-bearing element — labels disappear at that size. So icons are large (280px square), centered, and high-contrast. The flame-orange accent is the secondary read — even when the icon shape blurs, the orange dot says "this is bad desi plots."
