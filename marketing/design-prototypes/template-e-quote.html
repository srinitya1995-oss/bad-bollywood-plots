Template E — Meta Quote (Lateral Palette Cleanser)

No card art. Just the line, framed like it's hanging in a haveli alcove. Functions as the rest beat between card-heavy days. Ornament-heavy on purpose — when there's no card, the frame has to do all the work.

Composition

Triple frame: outer 1px gold rule, inner 1px gold rule (inset 38px), inner-inner dashed gold rule (inset another 14px). That's the "framed print on a haveli wall" reference. Crown top + bottom (mirrored): symmetric ornament bookends so the composition reads like a print, not a poster. Corner flourishes at 200px: larger than other templates because there's no card to anchor the eye — the corners do that work. Quote in 96px Playfair italic: per spec. The word "ruined" gets the gold accent — it's the load-bearing word in the joke. Gold halo, not BW halo: there's no card to belong to, so the glow goes gold (celebration of the line itself). Stamps top + bottom: "✦ unsolicited cinema review ✦" / "✦ baddesiplots.com ✦" — microcopy ornament.

Why ship this

Quotes are the highest-saving format on IG ratio for this kind of voice account. The card-voice rubric ("dry, cinematic, slightly unhinged") needs space to land without competing with plot text. Risk: 96px on 5 lines could overflow on shorter quotes — this one fits cleanly. Edge case: the longer quote variant should drop to 84px.
