Direction 1 — Maximalist Haveli

Heavy ornament density, jharokha arch dominant, gold leaf flourishes, theatrical. The card is an event — every petal placed.

Choices

Foreground: Card sits inside a real SVG jharokha arch with carved ogee curves, scalloped lobing, and a crown finial. Gold double-line stroke at 2px / 6px halo to feel like gilt leaf, not a CSS box. Midground: Paisley ornaments flank the arch on either side (real SVG, mehendi-derived). A pulsing BW glow halo sits behind the whole stack at 35% opacity (medium difficulty would be 35%; we're using easy here at slightly elevated opacity to push the BW signature for thumb-stop). Background: A 1300px mehendi medallion at 10% opacity gives the canvas atmospheric depth — you can't tell what it is, but the canvas isn't flat. Worn paper noise at 8% layered on top.

Type

Plot text in Playfair italic 54px (well above the 24px floor), tilted-curtain feel. Industry badge in DM Sans 14px tracked +0.4em. Crown stamp in 11px DM Sans bold tracked +0.5em — microcopy ornament, not body text.

Why ship this

It looks like a Bollywood poster pinned inside a haveli alcove. It is loud, ornate, and distinctly desi — no sterile minimalism, no banking-app energy. Risk: dense compositions can feel busy at 270px grid thumbnail; the arch silhouette compensates by giving an unmistakable shape signature.
