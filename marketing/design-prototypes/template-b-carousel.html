Template B — Carousel of 5

The saver. Cover and outro use full Direction-1 haveli treatment so the cover slide stops the thumb in grid view; inner slides 2–5 use Direction-2 modern editorial layout with a slim gold inset frame and corner ticks (subtle haveli accents on the borders, not full ornament density).

Composition

Slide 1 (cover): jharokha arch + crown + corner flourishes around a 104px Playfair italic headline. The numeral "5" gets the BW accent — the most cinematic word on the slide. Single ornament budget kept high because this is what shows in the grid. Slides 2–5: Direction-2 layout (industry kicker, gold quote-mark, ranged-left plot text), but with a slim gold inset frame at 48px and four corner L-ticks. That's the haveli accent on the border without competing with the plot. Slide-number pip in the footer ("02 / 05") replaces the CTA strip. Slide 6 (outro): haveli treatment again, gold URL band as the punctuation, "play 350+ more · link in bio" sub.

Accent words (BW)

DDLJ → "Punjab". Chak De! → "World Cup". 3 Idiots → "goat farm". Lagaan → "British Empire". Picked the most desi-specific or cinematic noun in each plot.

Risks

Six 1080×1350 slides at full ornament density = busy if user scrolls fast. The slim modern frame on inner slides is the rest beat. If the cover's accent numeral feels too dominant, drop to a 2px underline only.
