Template D — Vote / Opinion (BW vs TW)

The only template where the two glow families co-exist. Built as a strict left/right split with a gold "VS" disc punching through the dividing rule.

Composition

Two halos, sharply separated: BW halo lives at 24% horizontal center, TW halo at 76% — the dark valley between them is intentional, that's where the eye lands on the gold VS pip. Vertical gold rule with VS disc: the rule fades top and bottom into transparent so the divider doesn't fight the masthead or the footer; the disc has a 4px halo of bg color so it sits above the rule cleanly. Plot text shrinks to 32px: two 60–80 character plots side-by-side need this size to breathe. Quote mark drops from 220px to 140px to match. Vote buttons at the bottom of each panel: "BW it" / "TW it" in their respective family colors — these are the prompts users save for the comments.

Accent words

Chennai Express → "Tamil" (BW orange). RRR → "freedom fighters" (TW green). Both are the most desi-specific noun in their plot.

Caption pairing

"BW or TW? both got dance numbers. one made the Oscars. guess both 👇" — sets up the joke that one card is harder than it looks. Ships in caption, not on canvas.
