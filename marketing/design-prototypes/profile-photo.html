Profile photo — BP monogram in jharokha-arch crest

1080×1080 SVG inline. Designed legible at IG profile circle thumbnail (110px) — that means the BP monogram has to stop the eye, the arch is decorative bezel, and gold + flame are the only colors that survive at thumbnail size.

Choices

BP monogram, not full wordmark: "Bad Desi Plots" is too long for 110px circle. Stylized "BP" in Playfair Display 700 italic, the B in cream, the P in flame orange — primary brand recognition lives in the letter pair. Jharokha-arch silhouette: top half of the canvas wears the arch in gold, mehendi medallion at low opacity behind. Flame underline: a single flame-orange brushstroke under the monogram — the "officially bad" stamp microcopy. Inner ring: 2px gold ring at 90% radius gives the circle thumbnail a hard edge against IG's gray story-rings. No CSS-only ornaments: arch and medallion are real SVG.
