# Reply Bank — Week 1 (and beyond)

> Voice: dry, cinematic, slightly unhinged. Same register as the cards.
> Constraints: ≤2 sentences, witty, never founder-voice, never effusive.
> Reply within 60 minutes for the first 30 days (algo loves session length).
> Use 🔥 / 🌿 sparingly as industry-marker emojis. Skip every other emoji.

## Bonus templates (use sparingly)

- **Tagged a friend:** "your cousin will fumble this. screenshot the proof."
- **"5/5 on the carousel":** "5/5. your mom is forwarding this to the cousin group as we speak."
- **DM intro from a meme account:** "saw your page. 350+ unbranded cards yours to repost — DM us your handle."
- **"Make a hindi version" / language ask:** "currently English only. transliterations welcome in the cards. Hindi UI is on the list."

---

## Anti-replies (never ship these)

- ❌ "Thanks so much! 💕💕💕" — saas voice
- ❌ "Hahaha glad you loved it!" — too cheerful, breaks register
- ❌ "Great guess!" — sycophantic, generic
- ❌ "Stay tuned for more!" — corporate
- ❌ Any reply with three or more emojis
- ❌ Any reply that ends with an exclamation point unless it's earned
