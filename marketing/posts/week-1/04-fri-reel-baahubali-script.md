## A. ElevenLabs voice prompt (paste into voice gen)

**Recommended voice:** ElevenLabs "Adam" with Indian-English style override, or "Arjun" if available. Style preset: "Narration — Cinematic." Stability 35, Similarity 75, Style Exaggeration 60.

**Style direction (paste as voice instruction / system prompt):**

```
Read the following lines in the voice of an over-dramatic Bollywood movie-trailer
narrator. Slight Hindi-English accent — think 1990s VHS film opening. Unhurried.
Each sentence is its own beat. Long pauses between lines.

The comedy is that you are reading something absurd as if it is the most serious
thing in the world. Do not wink. Do not lighten the delivery. The plot is funny
because YOU are not.

Pacing:
- Line 1 ("Foundling raised by a waterfall tribe..."): start low, slow build,
  hold the word "tribe" half a beat longer than natural.
- Line 2 ("...discovers he might be royalty."): rise on "discovers", soften on
  "might", deadpan flat on "royalty".
- Line 3 ("Climbs the waterfall to confirm."): each word equally weighted. No
  emphasis. Like reading a grocery list.
- Line 4 ("Correct."): a full beat of silence first. Then drop it like a verdict.

Total reading time: 9 seconds. Do not rush.
```

**Script to read:**

```
Foundling. Raised by a waterfall tribe.

Discovers he might be royalty.

Climbs the waterfall.

To confirm.

Correct.
```

## B. Three alternate hooks (test the first 1.5 seconds)

The first 1.5 seconds is everything. Cut three variants of the opening, A/B/C the reach over the first 24h, kill the losers.

| Variant | Opening line / visual | Why this might work |
|---|---|---|
| **A — RECOMMENDED** | VO: *"Foundling..."* (held, low) over slow push on card. Emerald glow flares in. | Mirrors the card voice exactly. Trusts the audience. Cinematic. |
| **B** | On-screen text "guess this movie." Card already centered. VO starts at 0.4s. | Faster orient — explicit framing for new viewers. Lower drama. Better for cold IG audiences. |
| **C** | Cold open: thali clang at 0.0s, half-second flash of card back (movie name blurred), then card front slams in. VO starts at 1.0s. | Audio hook + tease. Best for TikTok/Reels FYP scrollers. Highest stop rate, possibly lower watch-through. |

**Recommendation: ship Variant A first.** It matches the brand voice. If reach is below benchmark by 24h, post Variant B as a separate cut on Saturday.

## C. Music + SFX cue points

**Bed:** Low harmonium drone, pitched to D minor. -18dB under VO. Royalty-free or AI-generated (Mubert, AIVA). No copyrighted Bollywood scores — algorithm penalizes and we don't have rights.

| t (s) | Cue | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| 0.0 | Harmonium drone fades in | -24dB → -18dB by 1.5s |
| 1.5 | Soft tabla heartbeat enters | Once every 1.5s, pulse with card glow |
| 6.5 | Drone subtly rises | +2dB lift under "Climbs the waterfall" |
| 8.5 | **Total silence** | Cut all music for the "Correct." beat. The silence sells it. |
| 9.5 | **THALI CLANG** | At the apex of the card flip. -3dB, sharp attack, no reverb. This is our signature SFX. |
| 9.5–11.0 | Drone + tabla return, swelling | |
| 11.0 | Shehnai swell soft enters under URL | Holds out to 12.0s, fades to silence in last 0.2s |
